    Why cardio is important?

    Cardiovascular exercise, or "cardio," refers to activities that elevate your heart rate and increase oxygen circulation throughout the body. It includes exercises like running, cycling, swimming, and jumping rope, which improve heart health, endurance, and calorie burn. Incorporating regular cardio sessions into your fitness routine can boost energy levels, improve mood, and support overall cardiovascular health.

    Benefits of Weight Traning

    Weight training involves lifting weights or using resistance to build strength, muscle mass, and endurance. By challenging muscles through exercises like squats, deadlifts, and bench presses, weight training helps improve bone density, metabolism, and overall functional fitness. Consistent weight training can lead to increased muscle tone, improved posture, and enhanced athletic performance.
